      • Credentials & Admissions

      During law school, Desiree actively engaged in community outreach by teaching law classes at local jails and juvenile detention centers, earning numerous accolades for her commitment to service. Initially practicing criminal defense, she expanded her focus to include civil litigation and civil rights law. As the supervising attorney at Ben Crump Law’s Las Vegas office, she leads a team of paralegals and manages a significant caseload with precision and compassion.

      Born in Atlanta, Georgia, and the youngest of four children, Desiree was inspired by her mother, Rev. Dr. Sharon G. Austin—the first female pastor at the historic Ebenezer Baptist Church. After relocating to Florida at a young age, she earned her bachelor’s degree in government from Berry College in 2014 and later received her law degree from St. Louis University School of Law in 2018.

      Biography

      Desiree Austin Holliday is a distinguished civil rights attorney with a proven record of defending individual rights and advocating for justice. With extensive expertise in wrongful death litigation, police misconduct, and employment discrimination, she combines strategic legal insight with an unwavering commitment to social justice.
